
Display image -> 
Play sound -> 
Display word

1. user first click the start button
2. word stage display
3. get the on-stage word from the word list
4. show the image of current word
5. after some buffer time, the sound play
6. show word when sound finish

option:
7. need user type the word.
   if correct, continue to next word
   otherwise, show word, go to the sound step.
